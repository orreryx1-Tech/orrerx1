FAQ

Common questions

Are prices live or simulated?
Starter uses GBM simulation — realistic war-calibrated price behavior, not live exchange data. Analyst and Command connect to Twelve Data for real prices via WebSocket on 36 symbols. Analyst gets 7 live days/month; Command is unlimited.
What AI powers the briefings?
Claude by Anthropic — the same model behind Claude.ai. It receives each war event with full geopolitical context (Hormuz status, DEFCON, current prices) and generates specific market impact analysis and investment recommendations in seconds.
Can AI actually predict stock prices?
AI provides scenario-based analysis, probability-weighted price targets, and structured investment frameworks — not guaranteed predictions. It synthesizes geopolitical context, historical patterns and market fundamentals to give you an edge, not certainty.
